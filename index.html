Meet our Head Chef

Hideo Kyoshi is the fifth generation son of the Kyoshi lineage and has served as Kyoshi Sushi's Head Chef for over 40 years. He's dedicated his life to continuing the family legacy and continues to serve to this day, passing down centuries-old recipes to the next generation of Kyoshi Chefs.

Our Dedication to Sustainability

The Kyoshi family believes that we need to work to live in harmony with the world around us, and as such we are strongly dedicated to ensuring that all of our ingredients are bought from sustainable sources.

Our Dedication to Tradition

Our recipes remain unchanged through the centuries. The sushi on your plate today is prepared the same as it was over two hundred years ago, and prepared with only the finest ingredients.
