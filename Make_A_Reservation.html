Note from the Business

"Kyoshi Sushi accepts a limited number of reservations for parties of six or fewer. We do not take reservations for large parties, Valentine’s Day, or Mother’s Day. Parties are seated in order of arrival based on party size and may not be combined. We will hold reservations for 15 minutes after your reservation time. Additionally, there are a few other off-peak times when we would be happy to accommodate your large party, so please call your local restaurant and speak to a manager. We will be happy to answer any questions and guide you to the best times to dine! "
